A Brief Explanation Of "Faceted Search Systems":

A Faceted Search System is a powerful way to search and filter information on a website. It helps users quickly find what they’re looking for by letting them narrow down results using specific categories, called facets. For example, in my Nike Shoe Store, you can filter the results by category(Women/Men/All), Availability(On Sale/In Stock), Price Range, Type, Model, Color, and Size. The shoes have different types, and each type has several specific models. When you select a shoe type, the corresponding models will be activated for you to choose from. You even can Sort the results based on their price — these are the FACETS. Additionally, there is a "Clear All Filters" button to reset the applied filters and start fresh. As you select options, the search results update to match your preferences, making it easier to find exactly what you need.
